Contribute

We invite you to contribute by writing for us regularly or publishing your article in our magazine.

Here are the requirements:

For us to publish your article, you have to send it to us for review. Please ensure you read the Writing Guidelines before sending it. The article should be at least 500 words long and have at least 3 high-quality images and not more than 5.

To join our writing team, please send us your résumé and an example article of 400 - 500 words. We will not publish or use your material without your permission. If you join the team you will be expected to write 1 article per week.

Please note that you will not be compensated in any way for contributing an article or writing for the magazine regularly.
